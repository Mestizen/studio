How we work

Our process.
No surprises.

  • 01

    Discovery & Brand Audit

    We dig into your business, market, and competitors. Understand where you are and map where you need to be. Honest assessment, clear direction.

  • 02

    Strategy & Positioning Workshop

    A focused session with you to define brand values, target audience, competitive differentiation, and the big idea that will anchor everything else.

  • 03

    Identity Design (2–3 directions)

    We present multiple visual directions — each fully developed, not just moodboards. You pick the direction, we refine it to perfection with two rounds of revisions.

  • 04

    System Build & Guidelines

    From the chosen direction, we build the full system: all logo variations, color tokens, type scale, patterns, and iconography. Delivered as a brand bible + production files.

  • 05

    Handoff & Brand Activation

    You leave with everything: editable source files, export-ready assets, guidelines PDF, and a walkthrough call so your team knows exactly how to use it.
