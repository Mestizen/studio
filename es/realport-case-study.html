El Brief

Conectando capital e infraestructura sostenible.

Confianza Estructura Flujo Innovación Transparencia

RealPort was a fintech startup building an investment platform on top of a European regulated blockchain and smart-contract framework. The platform solved a structural problem in the renewable energy market: small institutional investors lacked direct access to renewable asset owners, while many developers struggled to reach qualified capital partners.

RealPort created a trusted digital infrastructure connecting both sides — using regulated blockchain technology and smart contracts to enable transparent, secure, and efficient investment processes. Opening sustainable asset opportunities to investors who previously could not participate in these deals.

The brand identity was designed to reflect this role as a trusted bridge. The logotype construction illustrates the core concept of connection, structure, and flow — visually representing how RealPort links investors with sustainable assets through a robust digital framework.

01 — Sistema de Logo

Estructura, conexión, flujo.

The RealPort mark encodes the brand’s core concept — a regulated, trusted bridge between capital and renewable infrastructure. Every element carries meaning.

Un símbolo construido sobre confianza y geometría.

The hexagonal shield form references regulated security and stability — the foundation of any serious financial infrastructure. The R mark within references both the brand name and the routing of capital flows through the platform.
